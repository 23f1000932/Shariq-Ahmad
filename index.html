About Me

I am an award-winning educator and CTET Qualified (Paper I & II) specialist with a deep passion for Social Science and Economics. Recognised as the "Best Teacher" (Senior Section) for the 2025–26 session at Sri Haldhar School and College, I bring both academic excellence and real-world classroom impact to everything I do.

With over 5 years of formal teaching experience across secondary and senior secondary levels — plus a decade of independent volunteer tutoring since 2014 — I have honed a distinctive ability to make complex subjects like Macroeconomics, Civics, History, and Geography engaging and accessible. I leverage Smart Class systems and Digital Blackboards to create dynamic, technology-driven learning environments.

Beyond the classroom, I serve as an Academic Coordinator, overseeing faculty scheduling, curriculum alignment, and school-wide academic operations. I hold an M.Com from SHUATS and a B.Ed, and I am also proud to have cleared the AFCAT (Written) three times — a testament to my disciplined and goal-oriented mindset. I am fluent in Hindi and English, with elementary proficiency in Arabic.
